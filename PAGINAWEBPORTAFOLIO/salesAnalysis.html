Sales Analysis

This case study is based on a computer hardware business which is facing challenges in dynamically changing Indian market. Sales director decides to invest in data analysis project and he would like to build tableau dashboard that can give him real time sales insights.

SQL Query:

We obtained the data from a MySQL database, so we didn't want to alter it. Instead, we created queries to generate tables and exported them in .csv format to create the dashboards in Tableau.

Tableau:

For the Tableau part, we created different calculated fields within Tableau to generate columns with amounts in both USD and INR so that investors from the United States as well as India could easily visualize and understand the data.

Insights:

  • Analyzing the information presented in the dashboards, we can see that historically, sales tend to decrease at the beginning of the year and increase in the second half. Therefore, it is normal for sales numbers to decline up until the data we have (June 2020), especially considering the pandemic at that time.
  • We also observed that, percentage-wise, physical sales are much higher than e-commerce sales. Given the pandemic, we recommend investing in advertising through social media and the internet to boost online sales.
  • Furthermore, despite Delhi generating the highest sales, Surat and Patna show higher profit margins, both having 100% of their sales online. This reinforces our recommendation to focus on e-commerce.
