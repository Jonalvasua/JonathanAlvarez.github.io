Sales Dashboard

I created Sales and Customer Dashboards that provide an overview of sales metrics and customer behavior, enabling year-over-year comparative analysis and facilitating strategic decision-making.

Tableau:

  • Analysis of Trends and Annual Comparisons: Developed dashboards that display monthly trends for sales, profits, and quantities, comparing performance between the current and previous years. Additionally, I identified the highest and lowest sales months to highlight critical points.
  • Customer Segmentation and Performance: Implemented a customer dashboard that analyzes customer behavior, including distribution based on the number of orders and a ranking of the top 10 most profitable customers. Key metrics such as the number of orders and the date of the last order are highlighted.
  • Interactivity and Data Filtering: Designed interactive charts that allow users to filter data by product categories and geographic locations, as well as the ability to select historical years for detailed analysis.
